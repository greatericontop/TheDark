# Mechanics for Nerds

## Zombies

Normal Zombie

- First appears round 1
- 20 HP
- Low damage
- Fairly slow

Helmet Zombie

- First appears round 3
- 40 HP
- Low damage
- Fairly slow

Chainmail Armor Zombie

- First appears round 8
- 80 HP
- Medium damage
- Medium speed

Iron Armor Zombie

- First appears round 16
- 160 HP
- Medium-high damage
- Medium speed

Zombie Villager

- First appears round 20
- Must be killed within 30 seconds, otherwise it becomes enraged
- 350 HP
- High damage
- Medium speed

Zombie Piglin

- First appears round 30
- Must be killed within 45 seconds, otherwise it becomes enraged
- 900 HP
- Instant death if it hits you
- Medium speed

Piglin Brute

- First appears round 40
- Must be killed within 55 seconds, otherwise it becomes enraged
- 3,200 HP
- Instant death if it hits you
- Medium speed

Baby Dragon Zombie

- First appears round 43
- 400 HP
- High Damage
- Incredibly fast

Piglin Brute Final Boss

- Appears round 50
- Must be killed within 60 seconds, otherwise it becomes enraged
- 10,000 HP
- Instant death if it hits you
- Medium speed

## Weapons

Pistol: the basic starter weapon

- Max top path upgrade: **50 Cal**: deals high damage
- Max bottom path upgrade: **Machine Gun Pistol**: fires very fast

Rifle: built for high single-target damage

- Max top path upgrade: **Doom Rounds**: shots deal an incredible 100 damage
- Max bottom path upgrade: **Ultra Assault Rifle**: shoots 5 round bursts at a time with no cooldown

Shotgun: takes down groups with ease

- Max top path upgrade: **Wave of Destruction**: fires 39 shells at once
- Max bottom path upgrade: **Insane Buckshot**: shots do high damage and can pierce through multiple zombies

Flamethrower: lights zombies on fire

- Max top path upgrade: **Nether Master's Napalm**: high-damage fire lasts forever and causes burning zombies to take 25% more damage from all sources
- Max bottom path upgrade: **Perma-Flame**: no cooldown between shots

Midas Pistol: gives extra money

- Max top path upgrade: **Zombies to Gold**: 250% more coins per damage dealt
- Max bottom path upgrade: **Zombie Investments**: 500 coins per round and pays 3% interest on up to 5,000 coins

Rocket Launcher: shoots bombs that explode and deal area damage

- Max top path upgrade: **Nuclear Bombs**: bombs deal massive damage and hit many zombies
- Max bottom path upgrade: **Cluster Bombs**: rockets explode into 4 high-damage explosions on impact

Ice Blaster: slows zombies down

- Max top path upgrade: **Absolute Zero**: shots freeze zombies in place for 8 seconds
- Max bottom path upgrade: **Glacial Barrage**: shots 3 ice beams at once that deal high damage
